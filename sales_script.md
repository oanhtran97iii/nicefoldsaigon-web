# Sales Script for Nice Fold Saigon

## 1. Greeting
✨ Thank you for choosing Nice Fold Saigon! ✨
Please note that pickups are available today from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM only.
Please let us know your preferred service.

## 2. Top 10 customer questions and answers

1. "Do you pick up laundry from the hotel?"
   - Yes, we pick up directly from you or from the hotel reception. Please leave your laundry at reception or the agreed spot, and our driver will collect it. If the distance is over 6 km, an extra pickup/delivery fee may apply.

2. "How do you charge for the service?"
   - We have three packages:
     • Same-day Express: 180,000 VND for up to 5 kg, then 30,000 VND per extra kg.
     • 4-hour Express: 220,000 VND for up to 5 kg, then 35,000 VND per extra kg.
     • Standard 24h: 30,000 VND per kg, minimum 3.5 kg. If you have less than 3.5 kg, the price is 105,000 VND.
   - Pickup & delivery is 40,000 VND round trip. Separate white clothes are included for Express packages; for Standard, separate white wash is +30,000 VND.

3. "Do you include VAT?"
   - Yes, the Same-day Express and 4-hour Express prices already include 8% VAT.

4. "If I need it today, is it still possible?"
   - Yes, if you message us before 5 PM, we can usually handle Same-day Express and return your laundry around 7–9 PM, or finish it within 4 hours for 4-hour Express. If you contact us after 5 PM, the earliest delivery will be the next morning.

5. "Do you wash whites separately from colors?"
   - Yes. For Express orders, separate washing for white or light-colored clothes is included. For Standard 24h, we can wash whites separately for an additional 30,000 VND.

6. "How do I schedule pickup?"
   - I need:
     1️⃣ Your preferred pickup time
     2️⃣ Your name, room number, and hotel name
     3️⃣ Your pickup option: please leave the laundry at reception
     4️⃣ Google Maps link of your location (optional)
     5️⃣ Service type: Same-day Express, 4-hour Express, or Standard 24h, plus estimated weight
     6️⃣ Whether you have white or light-colored clothes that should be washed separately.

7. "How do I pay?"
   - Please place a 300,000 VND deposit inside your laundry bag and let me know when it’s ready. We accept cash only; any remaining balance will be returned when we deliver your laundry.

8. "Can you pick up after 5 PM?"
   - Our pickup and delivery time is from 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. If it’s too late today, we can schedule pickup for tomorrow morning.

9. "What if you are fully booked today?"
   - If we’ve already reached our online booking limit, I’ll ask you to reserve for tomorrow so we can still give your order proper attention.

10. "Can express service still be completed if I book after 5 PM?"
    - For express service after 5 PM, we likely cannot finish it today because our machines stop at 7:00 PM. In that case, the laundry will be delivered back around 12 PM tomorrow.
