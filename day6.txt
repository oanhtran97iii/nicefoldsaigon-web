* **Title**: Don't ruin your favorite clothes! Fabric care tips for smart travelers.
* **Content**:
Traveling light means packing your favorite outfits, but keeping them fresh can be tricky! To make sure your clothes last, here is a quick guide on what fabrics you should NEVER machine wash or dry at high temperatures:

❌ Wool & Cashmere: High heat causes wool fibers to shrink and felt, turning your cozy sweater into a doll-sized shirt.
❌ Silk: Extremely delicate and prone to water spots, color bleeding, and tearing in standard washers.
❌ Premium Linen: Hot drying will cause severe shrinkage and permanent creasing.

💡 Smart Travel Hack: Always check the laundry care label (the tiny tag inside your collar or side seam) before washing.
